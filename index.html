Key Insights

The "Silent" Drain

While the average loss is $170, the danger lies in detection. 23% of parents rarely or never check their child's card activity, meaning many small purchases often go unnoticed until they accumulate into a larger sum.

The Refund Gap

Despite consumer protections improving, approximately 60% of parents do not request a refund after an unauthorized charge, often assuming it's their own fault or that the process is too difficult.
